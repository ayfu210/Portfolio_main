lighthouse

USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN

Lighthouse is a LGBTQA+ exclusive safe house finder for youths organized and sponsored by local Vancouver youth services. It is designed to be a quick and efficient solution for LGBTQA+ youth 19 and under to find a safe, temporary environment to stay in if they are experiencing trouble at home, school, or anywhere else. Lighthouse was created by a team comprising of myself and 3 other students. Click the link below to view the final product, or keep scrolling to find out how we did it!

the problem

We started ideating with the goal of assisting youths with free resources where they can grow emotionally or educationally. Currently, there are no apps that offer resources exclusive to the LGBTQA+ community. We wanted to cater to them specifically, and help them seek refuge in their times of need.

what i learned

While this project pushed my research, wire framing, and interface design skills, the most valuable thing I learned was the new perspective I gained in regards to design and how it can be applied. By working with the LGBTQA+ and finding out about their needs and concerns, I really learned how to use empathy in my design decisions and design on a human level. I learned how to put myself in someone else's shoes and design for the user and practice instead of myself.
